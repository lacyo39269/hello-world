Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform for alternative artists to show off their work and be appreciated for it. We do not put down any form of art and are extremely open minded when it comes to the art posted on here.

What Our Art Is About

Artist: Mason Evans

Our art is made to stand out. Many artists featured on this website do not follow artistic norms. Our goal with art is to make people scared of our art and make people ask "why?" We want our art to stand out. However, though we want our art to take people by suprise, we also want our art to be respected and looked at like any other art work.

Creator: Lacy Hughes

"I have a history with being told not to make the art I enjoy because it makes people uncomfortable. That is why I wanted to make a platform for alternative artists where they will be respected and appreciated."
