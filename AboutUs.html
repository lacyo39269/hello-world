Why This Website Was Created

Artist: Jazminbean

Many new artists entering the art world in this day and age are unusual and make art that many are not fimilar with. That is why this website is very accepting and open minded to the new and different art that artist create. This is a platofrm that in not judgemental and will not shy away from any form of art.

Why This is Important

Artist: Reflectivedesire

It is important for there to be a platform for alternative artist. This is because so many teachers, adults, and those who don't understand this art form shy away from it. They are not used to this style and subject and this causes them to ignore it. However, these artists deserve the same amount of respect as anyone else.

Creator: Lacy Hughes

"I have a history with being told not to make the art I enjoy because it makes people uncomfortable. That is why I wanted to make a platform for alternative artists where they will be respectedand appreciated."
